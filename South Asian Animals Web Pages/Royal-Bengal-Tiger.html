Royal Bengal Tiger

The national animal of Bangladesh and India.

The Royal Bengal Tiger is known for its unique beauty and strength. It is the largest tiger species in the world and is found in the Sundarbans mangrove forest of Bangladesh and India. The Royal Bengal Tiger is a carnivorous animal and feeds on deer, wild boar, and other animals. The Royal Bengal Tiger is a solitary animal and is known for its hunting skills. The Royal Bengal Tiger is an endangered species and is protected by law in Bangladesh. The Royal Bengal Tiger is a symbol of strength and power in Bangladesh and is considered to be a national treasure.
