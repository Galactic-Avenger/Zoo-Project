Gharial

The Gharial is a species of crocodile found in the Indian subcontinent.

The Gharial is a very large crocodile and can grow up to 6 meters in length. It is a very powerful crocodile and is considered to be quite dangerous. The Gharial is unique among crocodiles because of its long, thin snout. The Gharial is a very powerful predator and can kill large animals like deer and wild boar. It is a very good swimmer and can be found near water bodies. Gharial is a very good hunter and can hunt at any time of the day or night. It is a very good ambush predator, but most likely it does not attack humans.
