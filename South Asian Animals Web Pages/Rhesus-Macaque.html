Rhesus Macaque

The most common monkey in South Asia.

The Rhesus Macaque is a medium-sized monkey with a pink face and a long tail. The Rhesus Macaque is a very social animal and lives in large groups. It is a very intelligent animal and is known to use tools to get food. The Rhesus Macaque is an omnivore and eats a variety of foods including fruits, vegetables, insects, and small animals. The Rhesus Macaque is a very adaptable animal and can be found in a variety of habitats including forests, grasslands, and urban areas. The Rhesus Macaque is a very good climber and spends most of its time in the trees. The Rhesus Macaque is a very vocal animal and communicates with other members of its group using a variety of calls and gestures.
