Nubian Ibex

The Nubian Ibex is a species of wild goat found in rocky mountains and arid landscapes. It is easily recognizable by its impressive curved horns and strong climbing abilities.

Ibexes are expert climbers using their sharp hooves and incredible balance to scale steep cliffs, where they can escape predators like wolves and leopards. They are highly adapted to desert life surviving on sparse vegetation and drinking water only when necessary.

Due to hunting and habitat loss Nubian Ibex populations are at risk. Conservation efforts aim to protect these beautiful creatures ensuring they remain a part of Middle Eastern wildlife for generations to come.
