Arabian Horse

The Arabian Horse is one of the oldest and most distinguished horse breeds in the world. It is known for its endurance, intelligence, and distinctive high tail carriage.

Historically, the Arabian Horse played a crucial role in warfare and transportation. Today, it is used in endurance racing, show jumping, and recreational riding.

Arabian Horses are prized for their friendly nature and close bonds with humans. Their genetic influence can be found in many modern horse breeds.
