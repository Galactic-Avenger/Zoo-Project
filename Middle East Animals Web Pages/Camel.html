Egyptian Vulture

The Egyptian Vulture (Neophron percnopterus) is a unique scavenger bird known for its intelligence. Unlike other vultures, it uses tools like rocks to crack open eggs, making it one of the few birds to exhibit tool usage.

It has a striking appearance with white feathers and a yellow face. Despite its adaptability, the species faces threats due to habitat loss, poisoning, and electrocution from power lines.

Conservation efforts focus on creating awareness about the importance of vultures in ecosystems. They play a crucial role in cleaning up dead animals and preventing the spread of disease.
